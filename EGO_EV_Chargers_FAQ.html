EGO EV Chargers - FAQ’s

What is the EGO EV Charger?

The EGO EV Charger is a single-phase, 7.2 kW home-based electric vehicle charger designed for fast and efficient charging of electric vehicles at home.

Who are the EGO EV Chargers designed for?

Our chargers are designed for homeowners with electric vehicles who want a reliable, efficient, and user-friendly home charging solution. They are also targeted at professional installers who seek high-quality, easy-to-install EV chargers for their clients.

What are the main features of the EGO EV Charger?

  • High power output of 7.2 kW
  • User-friendly interface
  • Compact and sleek design
  • Safety features including overcurrent and overvoltage protection
  • Durable and weather-resistant casing
  • Compliance with CE Marked EMC Directive 2014/30/EU, IEC 61851-1, and IEC 62196-2 standards

How long does it take to fully charge an EV with the EGO EV Charger?

Charging time varies depending on the vehicle's battery capacity, but generally, a 7.2 kW charger can add about 32-48 km of range per hour of charging.

What type of electric vehicles can be charged with the EGO EV Charger?

The EGO EV Charger is compatible with almost all electric vehicles on the market. Most vehicles and chargers are designed to be compatible with the industry-standard “Type 2” charging cables.

Yes, we recommend having the charger installed by a certified RECI electrician to ensure it is installed safely and correctly.

What safety features are in the EGO EV Charger?

The EGO EV Charger has integrated PEN Detection, a Residual Current Detector (RCD), and anti-tilt technology i.e., it will turn itself off if the unit tilts past a certain angle. PEN and anti-tilt features may not be set up as standard for your unit so ask your installer to have them configured if you want these features.

How quickly will my EV charge with the Ego EV Charger?

Naturally, charging speed depends on a number of factors. These include:

  • EV battery capacity
  • Power output from the charger to the EV

With a standard home single-phase (230V) power supply, the user can expect a maximum power output of 7.2kW. A commercial 3-phase power supply can supply a much greater maximum of 22kW of power. Naturally, this means that the 3-phase supplied charger will be much quicker. However, the single-phase charger is more than adequate for home charging.

The below table displays estimated charging times for a full charge for a pair of EVs with different battery sizes given a single-phase or 3-phase power input.

Power Rating 40kWh Battery 60kWh Battery
7.2kW 6h 30mins 9h 15mins
22kW 2h 20mins 3h 15mins

What makes an EV charger ‘Smart’?

Smart EV Chargers (such as the Ego EV Charging products) differ significantly from traditional EV chargers. They have a higher level of functionality where the user can monitor power usage, schedule charge sessions, manage power load and more. Configurable features can be accessed via a companion user mobile app.

What features can I expect for a commercial charging solution?

Our 3-phase chargers designed for commercial usage have a selection of added features when compared to home charging solutions:

  • 22kW available power
  • Group Load Balancing can be included to manage energy consumption between a group of EV charging stations.
  • Integrated RFID scanner technology allows for user authentication and payment (also available on single-phase version)
